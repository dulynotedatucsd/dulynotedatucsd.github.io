WHO ARE WE?

We are the seventh of nine a cappella groups found on campus at University of California, San Diego. Founded in Fall 2012, we've made strides to get to where we are today. Together, our members sing and perform, united by a love of music.

Comprised of nearly 20 talented students from UCSD, Duly Noted sings a diverse repertoire of music. Each year, the group sings new arrangements composed by our members. Duly Noted also hosts three yearly concerts, performs at on-campus events, attends festivals, and competes at the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella.

OUR MISSION

Members not only share a love for singing, but also for each other. Duly Noted is a spirited group of people who work hard to achieve the group's goals, and still have time to devote to their passions and studies. Some current and former members are successful programmers at Smule, Financial Analysts in SF, artists at AX, double majors, activists in our community, and Med School students. Duly Noted isn't just an a cappella group: it's an amazing place where dedication is key, friendships are made, and everyone has fun!
